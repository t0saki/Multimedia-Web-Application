Clothing platemaking

Basic information
Department:Hangzhou-West Lake District
Degree:Technical secondary school/secondary technology
Years of service:5-10 years

Job Responsibilities:
1. Make a sample that meets the requirements according to the requirements of the drawings and samples provided by the designer;
2. Responsible for guiding samplers to make samples.

Job requirements:
1. Familiar with the characteristics of various fabrics;
2. Be able to understand the basic design intention of the fashion designer, have a good team spirit and communication skills;
3. Have many years of experience in clothing plate making and cutting, and those who are engaged in stage clothing plate making are preferred;
4. Have a strong sense of professionalism, devote themselves to clothing plate making work, and be able to take on cutting work.
