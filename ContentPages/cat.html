Cat's Ears

Cat's Ears is a traditional Hangzhou snack. The dish is actually a kind of noodle, so named because of its resemblance to a cat's ear.

The noodle flap is shaped like a cat's ear, small and delicate, the diced ham is like onyx, the diced chicken is like amber, and the soup is fresh and tasty.
